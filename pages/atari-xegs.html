Atari XEGS

$5559 CAD

A hybrid powerhouse, the Atari XEGS (XE Game System) was a bold experiment that combined the power of an Atari 8-bit home computer with the plug-and-play simplicity of a game console. It was a versatile machine that could run a huge library of existing Atari computer games and software.

With its detachable keyboard and unique light gun, the XEGS was designed to be the complete package for both gaming and creativity. It's a unique and fascinating piece of Atari's history, perfect for collectors who love the ambitious spirit of the 80s.
