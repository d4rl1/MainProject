Sega Game Gear

$5329 CAD

Sega's answer to the Game Boy, the Game Gear was a handheld powerhouse that brought vibrant, full-color arcade experiences to the palm of your hand. With its horizontal grip and a backlit screen, it was a premium device designed for a superior visual experience.

Home to colorful versions of Sonic the Hedgehog and other Sega classics, the Game Gear was the must-have gadget for gamers who refused to settle for black and white. It's a bold piece of 90s tech and a symbol of the fierce console wars.
