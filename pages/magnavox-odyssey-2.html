Magnavox Odyssey²

$3219 CAD

The follow-up to a revolution, the Magnavox Odyssey² took the foundation of the original and expanded it in every way. It introduced a full keyboard, vibrant graphics, and a library of unique and educational game cartridges.

More than just a game machine, the Odyssey² was a "Master Strategy Series" of interconnected board games and video games, offering a deeper and more strategic experience. It's a unique and innovative piece of gaming history, perfect for collectors who love bold ideas.
