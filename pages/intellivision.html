Smarter than your average console, the Mattel Intellivision was the sophisticated choice for serious gamers. With its advanced 16-bit processor and a unique controller featuring a keypad, it offered deeper, more strategic games than the competition.

Famous for its impressive sports titles and exclusive licenses, the Intellivision was a true innovator. It's a key piece of video game history for collectors who appreciate a console that challenged players to think differently.
