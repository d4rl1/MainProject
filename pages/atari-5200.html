The ambitious follow-up to a legend, the Atari 5200 was a powerhouse built to bring the next level of arcade action home. Featuring enhanced graphics and a new controller design with a 360-degree joystick, this was Atari's high-tech vision for the future of gaming.

While its time was short, the 5200 is a fascinating piece of the Atari saga, offering some of the best-looking arcade ports of its era. It's a must-have for collectors who appreciate bold design and a pivotal moment in gaming history.
