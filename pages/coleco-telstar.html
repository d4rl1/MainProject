Before the console wars, there was the battle for the living room, and the Coleco Telstar was a champion. This series of machines dominated the era of "Pong clones" with a variety of models and a singular focus on fun.

With its iconic woodgrain paneling and built-in paddle controllers, the Telstar was a staple of 1970s home entertainment. It's a perfect symbol of the era when video games first captured the world's imagination, one simple and addictive match at a time.
