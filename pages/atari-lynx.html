The world's first color handheld, the Atari Lynx was a beast of a machine that was years ahead of its time. With its vibrant backlit screen, hardware-powered graphics, and an ambidextrous design for left-handed players, this was a portable powerhouse built for a premium arcade experience.

Ambitious, powerful, and undeniably cool, the Lynx is a true piece of gaming history for serious collectors who appreciate raw technological innovation.
